"You know the challenge with renting in Nairobi today..."

✗ "Agents take money from tenants and disappear."
✗ "Landlords wait months with vacant units."
✗ "Time-wasters who aren't serious."
✗ "Fake tenant references."
✗ "No transparency — you don't know who's in your property."

"That's exactly the problem Rentech solves."

Here's what we offer:

  ✅ FREE listing — professional photos, AI-optimized description
  ✅ Verified tenants — we screen employment and income
  ✅ WhatsApp inquiry system — inquiries come directly to you
  ✅ AI matching — we only send tenants who can actually afford you
  ✅ Fast — most landlords get serious inquiries within 72 hours
  ✅ 5% commission — only when the tenant moves in. Zero risk.

HANDLING OBJECTIONS:

OBJECTION 1: "I already have an agent."
RESPONSE: "Great — we work alongside agents too. But with us
you also get a digital listing, so tenants find you on Google
and WhatsApp. Most landlords on Rentech get their tenant faster
than through traditional agents, because we're reaching people
who are actively searching online right now. And you pay nothing
unless we succeed."

OBJECTION 2: "5% is expensive."
RESPONSE: "Consider this: one month's rent for a 2-bedroom in
Kilimani at KSh 65,000 — our commission is KSh 3,250. That's
less than a single phone bill. Compare that to traditional agents
who charge the TENANT — and then disappear. We only earn when
you earn. If no tenant, you pay nothing."

OBJECTION 3: "I don't trust online platforms."
RESPONSE: "That's a fair concern — and it's exactly why we
verify everything. We physically inspect properties before they
go live. We verify our landlords' IDs. And tenants are screened
before being sent to you. We can also show you our verification
process in detail. When can I come show you how it works?"

OBJECTION 4: "My property might rent itself."
RESPONSE: "If it's been vacant more than 3 weeks, every week
costs you the full rent value. That's KSh [X] lost forever.
Our typical landlord fills vacancies in 7–21 days. What's your
current vacancy status?"

OBJECTION 5: "I need the money upfront."
RESPONSE: "We understand. That's actually what makes Rentech
different from agents who ask for money upfront and deliver
nothing. With us it's reversed — we do ALL the work first,
and only invoice you after the tenant has moved in. You have
literally nothing to lose."
