❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding or listing a home on RENTECH.

How do you verify landlords?+
Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records and conducts a physical property inspection before any listing goes live. The green Verified badge means we have been there in person.
Is RENTECH free for tenants?+
100% free for tenants. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges — ever. You pay absolutely nothing to find your home on RENTECH.
How do I contact a landlord?+
Click the WhatsApp button on any listing. Your message goes directly to the verified landlord with the property details pre-filled. No middlemen, no delays.
How much does it cost to list a property?+
Basic listings are completely free. We earn a small commission only when a tenant successfully moves in. Premium (KSh 2,500/month) and Agency (KSh 8,000/month) plans are available for more visibility.
What areas do you cover?+
We cover 12 Nairobi areas: Kilimani, Westlands, Kasarani, South B, South C, Karen, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Parklands, Eastleigh, Lang'ata, and Ngong Road. More areas added monthly.
When is the mobile app launching?+
The Rentech mobile app for iOS and Android is currently in development and launching in 2026. Click the "Notify Me" buttons on the app section above and we'll WhatsApp you the moment it goes live.
