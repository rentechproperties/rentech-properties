❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding or listing a home on RENTECH.

How do you verify landlords?+
Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records with land registries and conducts a physical property inspection before any listing goes live. The green Verified badge means we have personally been to that property.
Is RENTECH completely free for tenants?+
Yes — 100% free for tenants, always. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges whatsoever. You pay absolutely nothing to find your home on RENTECH. We earn only from landlords on successful move-ins.
How do I contact a landlord?+
Click the WhatsApp button on any listing. Your message goes directly to the verified landlord with the property details pre-filled. No middlemen, no waiting — just a direct conversation with the property owner.
How much does it cost to list a property?+
Basic listings are completely free. We earn a small commission only when a tenant successfully moves in through RENTECH. Premium plans (KSh 2,500/month) and Agency plans (KSh 8,000/month) are available for more visibility and AI tools.
What areas in Nairobi do you cover?+
We currently cover 12 Nairobi neighbourhoods: Kilimani, Westlands, Kasarani, South B, South C, Karen, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Parklands, Eastleigh, Lang'ata, and Ngong Road. We are expanding to new areas monthly — contact us to request your area.
When is the RENTECH mobile app launching?+
The RENTECH app for iOS and Android is currently in development and launching in 2026. Click the "Notify Me" buttons in the app section above and we'll WhatsApp you the moment it goes live on the App Store and Google Play.
