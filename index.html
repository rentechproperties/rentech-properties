❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RENTECH Properties.

How do you verify landlords? +
Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records and conducts a physical property inspection before the listing goes live. Verified badge = we've been there in person.
Is RENTECH free for tenants? +
100% free for tenants. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges — ever. You pay nothing to find your home on RENTECH.
How do I contact a landlord? +
Click the WhatsApp button on any listing. Your message goes directly to the verified landlord with the property details pre-filled. No middlemen, no delays.
How much does it cost to list a property? +
Basic listings are completely free. We earn a small commission only when a tenant successfully moves in through RENTECH. Premium listing tiers are also available for higher visibility.
What areas do you cover? +
We currently cover 12 Nairobi areas: Kilimani, Westlands, Kasarani, South B, South C, Karen, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Parklands, Eastleigh, Lang'ata, and Ngong Road. More areas coming soon.
Can I pay rent through RENTECH? +
Currently rent is paid directly between tenant and landlord via M-Pesa. We are building an integrated M-Pesa escrow feature for additional security — launching soon.
