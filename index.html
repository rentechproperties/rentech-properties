Anab Osman is a serial entrepreneur, philanthropist and cultural anthropologist who has spent decades building bridges between communities and commerce across East Africa. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Sure Source charge for its services?
We operate on a transparent fee model — either a percentage of procurement value or a fixed project retainer depending on scope. All fees are agreed in writing upfront. No hidden charges, ever.
How quickly can you source a product I need?
For most standard products, we provide competitive quotes within 24–48 hours. Complex or specialized sourcing typically takes 3–7 business days depending on specifications and supplier availability.
Do you work with NGOs and grant-funded projects?
Yes — this is one of our specialties. We have extensive experience with donor compliance requirements, grant-funded procurement processes and the documentation standards required by international organizations.
Can you support government tender applications?
Absolutely. We support the full tender lifecycle including technical proposals, supplier documentation, bid management and compliance checks across national and county government levels in Kenya.
What is your minimum order value?
We work with organizations of all sizes. For direct procurement management we typically start from KES 100,000. Advisory and consultancy services are available for smaller organizations as well.
Do you operate outside Kenya?
Yes. We serve clients across Kenya, Somalia, Uganda, Tanzania, Ethiopia and Rwanda with supplier networks extending to UAE, China, India, Europe and the Americas.
How are your suppliers verified?
Every supplier undergoes a 7-point vetting process including business registration verification, quality certifications check, financial due diligence, reference checks and site visits where applicable.
