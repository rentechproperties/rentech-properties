Everything you need to know about renting with Rentech

How do you verify landlords?
+

Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records with government databases and conducts a physical property inspection. We also run AI scam detection on every submission before it goes live.

ID Verification Title Deed Check Physical Inspection
Is Rentech free for tenants?
+

Yes 100% free for tenants! No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges. You deal directly with the verified landlord. We only earn a 5% commission from the landlord when a tenant moves in successfully.

Tenant Promise: You never pay us a single shilling.
How do I contact a landlord?
+

Click the WhatsApp button on any listing to connect directly with the verified landlord. No forms, no middlemen, no delays. You can also:

  • Call landlord directly via WhatsApp
  • Schedule viewings instantly
  • Get quick responses (avg 24hr)
Can I list my property today?
+

Yes! Click "List Property Free" and complete our 4-step form in just 10 minutes. Your listing goes live within 24 hours after verification.

+

Report it immediately via WhatsApp (+254 723 783 337). We investigate within 2 hours and remove the listing. Zero tolerance policy.

Only fully verified, licensed agents with valid Nairobi business registration. No anonymous agents. Every listing shows the verified landlord or agent's identity clearly.
