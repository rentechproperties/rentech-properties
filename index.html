❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding or listing a home on RENTECH.

How do you verify landlords?+
Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records with land registries and conducts a physical property inspection before any listing goes live. The green Verified badge means we have been there in person.
Is RENTECH free for tenants?+
100% free for tenants. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges — ever. You pay absolutely nothing to find your home on RENTECH.
How do I contact a landlord?+
Click the WhatsApp button on any listing. Your message goes directly to the verified landlord with the property details pre-filled. No middlemen, no delays — just a direct conversation.
How much does it cost to list a property?+
Basic listings are completely free. We earn a small commission only when a tenant successfully moves in through RENTECH. No upfront costs, no monthly fees. Premium listing tiers are available for higher visibility.
What areas do you cover?+
We cover 12 Nairobi areas: Kilimani, Westlands, Kasarani, South B, South C, Karen, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Parklands, Eastleigh, Lang'ata, and Ngong Road. More areas are added monthly.
Can I pay rent through RENTECH?+
Currently rent is paid directly between tenant and landlord via M-Pesa. We are building an integrated M-Pesa escrow feature for additional security and dispute resolution — launching soon.
