❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RENTECH.

How do you verify landlords?+
Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks with land registries and conducts a physical property inspection before any listing goes live. The green Verified badge means we have personally been to that property.
Is RENTECH free for tenants?+
Yes — 100% free for tenants, always. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges whatsoever. You pay nothing to find your home on RENTECH. We earn only from landlords on successful move-ins.
How do I contact a landlord?+
Click the WhatsApp button on any listing. Your message goes directly to the verified landlord with the property details pre-filled. No middlemen, no waiting — just a direct conversation with the property owner.
How much does it cost to list a property?+
Basic listings are completely free. We earn a small commission only when a tenant successfully moves in. Premium plans (KSh 2,500/month) and Agency plans (KSh 8,000/month) give more visibility and AI tools.
What areas in Nairobi do you cover?+
We cover 12 Nairobi areas: Kilimani, Westlands, Kasarani, South B, South C, Karen, Lavington, Kileleshwa, Parklands, Eastleigh, Lang'ata, and Ngong Road. We are expanding to new areas monthly.
Is RENTECH compliant with Kenya's data laws?+
Yes. RENTECH complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and GDPR principles. We collect only necessary data, never sell personal information, and provide users with full rights to access and delete their data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
When is the mobile app launching?+
The RENTECH app for iOS and Android launches in 2026. Tap "Notify Me" on the app section above and we'll WhatsApp you the moment it goes live on the App Store and Google Play.
