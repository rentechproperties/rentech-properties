❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify landlords?+

Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records and conducts a physical property inspection. We also run AI scam detection on every submission before it goes live.

Is Rentech free for tenants?+

Yes — 100% free for tenants. No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges. You deal directly with the verified landlord.

What happens if I find a scam listing?+

Report it immediately via WhatsApp (+254 723 783 337). We investigate within 2 hours and remove the listing. Zero tolerance policy. We'll also help you find an alternative.

How do I apply for a property?+

Click the WhatsApp button on any listing to connect directly with the verified landlord. Our AI bot guides you through the application — no forms, no printing, all on WhatsApp.

Can I list my property today?+

Yes! Click "List Property Free" and complete our 4-step form. Your listing goes live within 24 hours after verification. Zero upfront cost — we only earn 5% on confirmed move-in.

Do you work with agents?+

Only fully verified, licensed agents with valid Nairobi business registration. No anonymous agents. Every listing shows the verified landlord or agent's identity clearly.

How does M-Pesa deposit work?+

Once you've agreed with the landlord, the deposit is paid directly via M-Pesa. Rentech facilitates and confirms — both parties receive a payment confirmation for security.

Which areas in Nairobi do you cover?+

All of Nairobi — from Eastleigh to Karen, Kasarani to South C, Westlands to Embakasi, and everywhere in between. 20+ areas listed and actively growing.
