Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify landlords? +

Every landlord submits their National ID and property title deed. Our team cross-checks records with government databases and conducts a physical property inspection. We also run AI scam detection on every submission before it goes live.

Is Rentech free for tenants? +

Yes! 100% free for tenants! No application fees, no viewing fees, no hidden charges. You deal directly with the verified landlord. We only earn a 5% commission from the landlord when a tenant moves in successfully.

How do I contact a landlord? +

Click the WhatsApp button on any listing to connect directly with the verified landlord. No forms, no middlemen, no delays. You can call, chat, and schedule viewings instantly.

Can I list my property today? +

Yes! Click "List Property Free" and complete our 4-step form in just 10 minutes. Your listing goes live within 24 hours after verification. Zero upfront cost — we only earn 5% on confirmed move-in.

What if I find a scam listing? +

Report it immediately via WhatsApp (+254 723 783 337). We investigate within 2 hours and remove the listing. Zero tolerance policy. We'll personally help you find an alternative verified property at no cost.
